Why JoyScroller?

JoyScroller is designed for anyone who wants to replace endless doomscrolling with intentional joyscrolling. By surfacing your own bookmarks right when you open a tab, it turns a reflex into a mindful, productive habit.

Daily Highlight

Each day, JoyScroller pins one random bookmark at the top of your feed — a small spark of serendipity to rediscover something you once cared about.

Folders & Reading Lists

Power your feed with your folders and reading lists, which get automatically synced when you download the extension. You can also click refresh icon to sync new bookmarks.

Quick Add

Found something worth saving? Right-click any page and add it instantly to your JoyScroller feed — no messy bookmark menus required.

Progress Marking

Mark links as read and watch them fade away. Your feed feels finite, giving you a sense of closure instead of an overwhelming backlog.

Ready to JoyScroll?

Stop doomscrolling. Start rediscovering the content you actually care about. JoyScroller makes every new tab a step toward meaningful reading, learning, and focus.
